Our Story

Bringing Ghana
to Your Doorstep

Multiple Mix was born from a simple frustration: finding authentic Ghanaian products outside of Ghana was either impossible or came with a serious compromise on quality.

We partner directly with kente weavers in Bonwire and food producers in Accra and Kumasi — no middlemen, no compromises. Every purchase supports a real artisan or small producer in Ghana.

The Name

The name Multiple Mix reflects what we are: a mix of cultures, categories, and communities. Fashion and food. Ghana and the world. Traditional and modern.

We believe these worlds don't have to be separate — and our store is proof of that.
